Grade 5
FAST Mathematics
Sample Test Materials
The purpose of these sample test materials is to orient teachers and students
to the types of paper-based FAST Mathematics questions. By using these materials,
students will become familiar with the types of items and response formats
they may see on a paper-based test. The sample items and answers are not
intended to demonstrate the length of the actual test, nor should student
responses be used as an indicator of student performance on the actual test.
The sample test materials are not intended to guide classroom instruction.
